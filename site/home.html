Welcome to Lytham Chess Club

We're glad you've found us. And no matter your skill level, we're happy to have you.

Join Us

We believe chess is for everyone

We meet on Thursdays at 7:15pm, at the Lytham Ex Service Club on Chapel Street in Lytham.

And no matter how seriously you take your chess, you're welcome here. Our players range from beginners to experts, and we're always happy to welcome new members, whether you want to play in competitions or just for fun.

Competitions

If you’re interested in competing, why not join one of our five teams? Each team caters to a different skill level. Team 5 is ideal for more casual players, or anyone looking to enter their first competition, whereas team 1 is reserved for our top players.
